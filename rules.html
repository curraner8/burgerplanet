RULES

The aim of the game is to survive on this planet where it is raining burgers(yellow). If you reach a score of 1000, you win.

Every time a falling burger hits you, you will get fatter and slower.

There is a weight(red) at the end of the map, once you touch it you will begin to shrink to normal size and return to normal pace.

Reaching this weight however, is a tricky task, as you are faced with obstacles....

And of course we can't forget that you are trespassing on this planet, so there will be enemies zooming across the map in an attempt to kill you.

Have Fun.
